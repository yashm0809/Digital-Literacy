Stay Safe Online: Comprehensive Prevention Checklist

1.PIN Rule: Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money. PINs are exclusively for sending money.

2.Verify Collect Requests: Only approve payment requests from known, trusted sources. If you are selling, you do not need to perform any action on the app to "receive" funds.

3.Use Official Channels: Always report suspicious activity or financial fraud to the National Cyber Crime Helpline at 1930 or the official portal at [suspicious link removed].

4.Privacy Settings: Set your social media profiles to private and be cautious about sharing personal contact details publicly.

5.Multi-Factor Authentication: Enable 2FA on all banking, email, and social media accounts to add a layer of security.

6.Secure Connections: Avoid conducting financial transactions over public, unsecured Wi-Fi networks (e.g., in cafes or airports).

7.URL Verification: Always check the website URL. Ensure it starts with https:// before entering any sensitive information.

8.Software Hygiene: Regularly update your smartphone’s operating system and payment apps to ensure the latest security patches are installed.
